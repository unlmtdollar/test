Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about NUUR token

What is the total supply of NUUR?

The total supply of NUUR tokens is fixed at 10,000,000. There will be no additional minting beyond this cap.

How can I participate in the presale?

Visit our presale page, connect your Sidra Chain compatible wallet, specify the amount you wish to contribute, and confirm the transaction.

When will tokens be distributed?

Tokens will be distributed immediately after the presale concludes and before exchange listings begin.

What wallets are supported?

Any wallet that supports the Sidra Chain network, including MetaMask (with custom RPC), Trust Wallet, and other EVM-compatible wallets.

Is there a vesting period for team tokens?

Yes, team tokens are subject to a 12-month linear vesting period to ensure long-term commitment to the project.

What utilities does NUUR have?

NUUR will power various dApps in the ecosystem including DeFi protocols, NFT marketplaces, gaming platforms, and governance mechanisms.
